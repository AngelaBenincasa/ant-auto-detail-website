Our Mission Statement

You put your car through a lot,
so why not treat your car to a spa day?

A Nice Touch Auto Detail, LLC is a luxury detail company that serves the greater New Haven area. Exposure to road grime, tree sap, bird droppings and other pollutants in the environment, deteriorate vehicle finishes. The best way to help prevent damage is with regular care and maintenance. Our interior and exterior spa packages will return your car like new condition. A Nice Touch Auto Detail, LLC has the expertise, training, and top of the line cleaning products to safely protect the finish of your vehicle and enhance the shine.
Leave the detailing to us!
